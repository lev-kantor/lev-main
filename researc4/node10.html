Physical adsorption

At the beginning of my scientific career, I was involved in calculations on the physical adsorption of water molecules on alkali halide surfaces using ab initio inter-atomic potentials. Special attention was paid to finding cooperative effects as the coverage increases (from a single molecule to several layers of them) which were assumed to be associated with different speed of solvation of the crystals in water [B1,B2, B8,B9] observed experimentally.
