A note on calculating electronic DOS

The calculation of the total/local/projected DOS is performed using the method of tetrahedra. In this method the whole Brillouin zone (BZ) is split into tetrahedra, the energies at the vertices of which are to be calculated by the DFT code. These energies are collected in a file band.out (Sections 4 - 6) and then (together with the information contained in the file brill.dat written by tetr) are used by lev00 to calculate the DOS.
