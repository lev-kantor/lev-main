traj_fmt

Key to specify the format of the trajectory file.

Options:

  • 0 - Don't record trajectory
  • 1 - Ymol xyz-style formal
  • 2 - XMOL (xmakemol) xyz-style format
  • 3 - DLPOLY (HISTORY) style format
Default: 1

Example: traj_fmt 1
