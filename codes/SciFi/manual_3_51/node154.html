extrapolate{F/T,polyn,points}

This option is supposed to control extrapolation of the tip force - not yet implemented. The first parameter is F (do not apply) or T (do apply extrapolation mechanism to the tip force), then the order of a polynomial to be used for the extrapolation, and , finally, the number of points .

Parameters: F, ,    or   T, ,

Default: T,2,3

Example: Fextrapolate{F/T,polyn,points} T 2 3
