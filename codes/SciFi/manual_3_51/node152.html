Atom_Sphere{#,dist}

Paramaters for placing the vdW sphere within the atomistic part; only needed if vAFM and SciFi are to work together. Should specify two parameters: (i) atom number which is used to measure the distance to the vdW sphere, , and the distance (in ) to the bottom of the vdW sphere from this atom.

Parameters: ,

Default: 1, 0.0

Example: Atom_Sphere{#,dist 15 1.25
