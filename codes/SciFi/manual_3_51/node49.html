shells

Controls whether shells in regions ``cluster atoms'' and ``tip atoms'' are allowed to relax.

Options:

  • moveall - shells of all atoms are allowed to relax; this option is useful, for instance, if one wants to calculate the electronic polarisation alone keeping all atoms in their initial positions;
  • frozenall - all shells are frozen irrespective of whether cores are allowed to move or not; this would correspond to the frozen core approximation if initial positions of shells are the same as for their cores;
  • follow - only shells of frozen cores are frozen; shells of atoms allowed to move will follow them; this is likely to be the most useful and frequently used option.
Default: moveall

Example: shells moveall
