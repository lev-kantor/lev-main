Start{X,Y}

Sets initial coordinates and for the tip (with respect to the force field file). Zero values correspond to the first point of the force field file.

Parameters: ,

Default: 0.0 0.0 (in m)

Example: Start{X,Y} 1.0d-10 -1.0d-10
