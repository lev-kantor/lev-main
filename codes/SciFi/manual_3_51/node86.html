algorithm

Specifies the algorithm used to integrate the equations of motion.

Options:

  • nve - Verlet Leapfrog
  • ber_nvt - Verlet Leapfrog with Berendsen thermostat
  • evn_nvt - Verlet Leapfrog with Gaussian thermostat
  • hov_nvt - Verlet Leapfrog with Nose-Hoover thermostat
  • stochastic - Verlet Leapfrog with atoms subject to stochastic thermostat. If this option is selected, friction coefficients must be provided in the .<Friction> box (section 8.5.3).
Default: nve

Example: algorithm stochastic
