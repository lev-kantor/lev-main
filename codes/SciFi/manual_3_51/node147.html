Lattice{/A1(xy),/A2(xy)}

Components along and axes of the laboratory Cartesian system of the lattice vectors and are given (in Å) in two lines coming next after the keyword: , on the first and , on the second.

Default: none

Example: Lattice{/A1(xy),/A2(xy)}

1.0 0.0

0.0 1.0
