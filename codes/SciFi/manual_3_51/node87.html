shells

Specifies the type of shell model to be used in dynamical simulations.

Options:

  • dynamic - fictitious shells dynamics will be used: shells given mass and are moved together with cores;
  • relax - only cores are dynamically moved whereas shells are relaxed at every timestep
Default: dynamic

Example: shells dynamic
